I started hand painting tokens around 2020. They were never intended for anything but personal use.

However, many people began asking me for commissions. Since I found it awkward evaluate the price of my original art I decided to start printing them and sell the copies instead.

And here we are.

Besides making tokens, I work as a Sound Designer for video games and enjoy playing Magic. My favorite format is Legacy, and my favorite deck is Painter.
